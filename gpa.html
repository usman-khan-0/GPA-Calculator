How Does This Work?

Your Grade Point Average (GPA) is calculated by dividing the total number of grade points you've earned by the total number of credit hours you've attempted.

GPA Formula
GPA = Σ(Grade Points × Credits) ÷ Total Credits

Example Calculation

Calculus II A- (3.7) 4 Credits
Physics I B+ (3.3) 3 Credits
Intro to Programming A (4.0) 3 Credits

Quality Points:

(3.7 × 4) + (3.3 × 3) + (4.0 × 3) = 36.7

Total Credits: 10

GPA: 36.7 ÷ 10 = 3.67
